Lasagne Recipe

Description

A lovely lasagne for you an your family to eat, if you can muster the energy and motivation to cook since the passing of your old dear friend Karlos The Crocodile

Ingredients

  • 500g Cheese
  • 500g Mince
  • 2 Cans of Peeled Plum Tomatos
  • 200g Mushrooms (chopped)
  • 140ml Vegetable Stock

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 240C
  2. Fry mince until brown
  3. Add mushrooms and sautee until golden
  4. Add peeled plum tomatos and break any large pieces of tomato to form a sauce
  5. Pour in vegetable stock and stir
  6. Turn down the heat and leave to simmer for 15mins
  7. Arrange cheese, pasta and sauce so it all looks like a bloody lasagne, godammit!
  8. Cook in the oven until you can't wait any longer OR when the cheese is goopy and that.
